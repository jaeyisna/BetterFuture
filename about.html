About BetterFuture

BetterFuture is a free, community based tutoring service for students who need extra help in school subjects. BetterFuture is a tutoring program that offers free academic assistance for elementary level to highschool level students. This website allows not just students, but also parents to learn about available subjects, view schedules, and register for basic education tutoring sessions.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide equal academic opportunities to students in the local community. We understand that not all students are able to progress at the same pace, and some may need additional guidance outside regular class hours. BetterFuture aims to bridge that gap by offering structured and supportive weekend tutoring sessions.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every student has access to free academic support and feels confident in their ability to succeed in school. Through volunteer dedication and community involvement, BetterFuture hopes to inspire students to develop a positive relationship with learning.

Who We Serve

  • Elementary students who need help with foundational subjects
  • High school students preparing for exams and academic requirements
  • Parents looking for structured weekend academic support

How Our Program Works

BetterFuture operates during weekends to accommodate students’ school schedules. We offer both online tutoring sessions and face-to-face sessions at our designated community center. Students must register in advance to ensure proper scheduling and volunteer tutor assignment.

Program Limitations

Since BetterFuture is a volunteer-based program, the number of available tutors and subjects may vary depending on availability. Tutoring services are free of charge but require proper registration and scheduling.
