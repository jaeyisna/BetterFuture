Subjects Offered

BetterFuture provides tutoring support in core academic subjects to help students strengthen their understanding and improve school performance.

Math

Step-by-step guidance in algebra, arithmetic, geometry and problem solving.

English

Reading comprehension, grammar improvement, and writing skills.

Science

Basic biology, chemistry and scientific concepts.

Mathematics Topics Offered

Our volunteer tutors can assist students with the following mathematics topics:

  • Basic Arithmetic (Addition, Subtraction, Multiplication, Division)
  • Fractions and Decimals
  • Basic Algebra Concepts
  • Geometry Fundamentals
  • Problem Solving Strategies

English Topics Offered

Students can receive tutoring assistance in the following English areas:

  • Reading Comprehension
  • Grammar and Sentence Construction
  • Vocabulary Development
  • Essay Writing
  • Basic Public Speaking

Science Topics Offered

Science tutoring includes guidance in these subjects:

  • General Science Concepts
  • Basic Biology
  • Introduction to Chemistry
  • Earth Science
  • Scientific Method and Experiments

Important Notes

  • Tutoring sessions are available during weekends only.
  • Both online and face-to-face sessions are offered.
  • Registration is required prior to attending sessions.
